Nice To Meet You

My name is Joshua Comstock, I am a current student at American Public University working towards my BS in Computer Science. Computers have intrigued me for almost as long as I can remember. About four years ago I had a good friend express their interest in computers, since they remembered I had always had the passion to learn about them. It was that day I was introduced to the first resource I am going to share with you all on this website. This website is called W3Schools. This was the first step I took in learning about coding and computers. I spent some time learning some code from their free online tutorials. After about a year of on and off coding, I hit a point where I thought it was just to difficult and took a break for a while. However, again thanks to this friend I had, he had me get into an introductory Python course at a local community college. This class is what finally solidified my passion for computers and coding. Since then, I have been scavenging the internet and people for information on free resources to teach code and computers. I have spent a lot of time finding all these free resources so I thought why not make a website where beginner computer geeks can find a bunch of helpful information all in one place!

Experience

Currently I am studying for an AS in computer technology which I will then transfer over to a BS in computer science. I have received coding certifications in HTML and CSS through W3Schools and an currently working on my JavaScript certification. I have also taken classes on Python, PHP, SQL, and some C. I have also enjoyed tinkering with my Arduino Uno and Cisco Packet Tracer. Eventually I hope to be proficient with networking and full stack development in order to land a decent job in the tech industry after I transition out of the military.
